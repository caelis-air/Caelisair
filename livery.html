Caelis Air Liveries

Dress your aircraft in the Caelis Air colours. Download our official liveries and fly the fleet in style.

How to Install

1
Download the livery

Click the Download button and grab the file from FlightSim.to.

2
Extract the files

Unzip the downloaded file to get the livery folder inside.

3
Move to Community folder

Place the folder in your MSFS 2024 Community folder.

4
Select in-sim

Launch MSFS 2024, select your aircraft, and find the Caelis Air livery in the livery list.
