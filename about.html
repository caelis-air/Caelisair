Our Story

About Caelis Air

Caelis Air is a global virtual airline community built on freedom, exploration, and connection. No limits. No routes. Just the sky.

Mission Statement

Why We Exist

Caelis Air exists to bring flight simulation enthusiasts together — not through rigid rules or mandatory schedules, but through a shared love of aviation and exploration. We believe there is no single right way to enjoy flight simulation.

Whether you're a seasoned virtual pilot with thousands of hours or someone who just installed MSFS for the first time, you belong here. Every destination has a story, and every pilot has a journey worth sharing.

"Fly what you want, when you want, where you want."

— The Caelis Air Promise

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🌐

Freedom

No mandatory routes, no required schedules. Fly what inspires you — a bush plane over the Rockies, a widebody across the Pacific, or laps around your hometown airport.

🧭

Exploration

Every destination has a story waiting to be discovered. We encourage pilots to explore beyond the major hubs and find the beauty in every corner of the simulated world.

🤝

Community

Flights are better when shared. Caelis Air is first and foremost a community — a place to swap stories, share screenshots, and celebrate each other's milestones.

📖

Learning

Every pilot starts somewhere. We welcome beginners and veterans alike, and believe that sharing knowledge makes us all better aviators.

🤍

Respect

Everyone is welcome regardless of experience level, background, or preferred aircraft. Caelis Air is a positive, inclusive space for all flight sim enthusiasts.

How It Started

One Pilot, One Idea

Caelis Air started with a simple frustration — most virtual airlines felt more like a second job than a hobby. Mandatory routes, strict schedules, rigid requirements. The fun of just flying wherever the wind takes you had been lost somewhere along the way.

So Caelis Air was built differently. A VA where the only rule is to enjoy yourself. Log your flights, earn your ranks, and be part of a community that celebrates the love of aviation above everything else.

We're just getting started — and the skies are wide open.
